Research & Development

Pioneering the
Infrastructure of Tomorrow.

At Titan Global, innovation is not an isolated department; it is the engine of our transition. Through our three dedicated research branches, we are reinventing materials, accelerating prop-tech startups, and designing the resilient cities of the future.

Startup Incubator & Prop-Tech

The Vanguard Project

The Vanguard Project is Titan Global's foresight platform and startup incubator. We identify, fund, and scale the most disruptive technologies in construction, from AI-driven site monitoring to autonomous heavy machinery.

Robotics & Automation

Integrating exoskeletons and automated bricklaying drones to increase safety and productivity on complex sites.

Vanguard Fund ($500M)

Our dedicated venture capital arm investing strictly in early-stage climate-tech and construction-tech innovators.

Scientific Partnerships

Lab Recherche Environnement

A unique partnership between Titan Global and leading global universities. The Lab exists to bridge the gap between pure academic research and scalable, commercial construction materials.

Bio-Concrete

Self-healing materials

CO₂ Trap

The Future of Cities

Urban Transitions

Our in-house think tank dedicated to anticipating the macro-trends of urbanization. We bring together urban planners, sociologists, and mobility experts to design the master-planned communities of 2050.

    Dynamic Charging Infrastructure
