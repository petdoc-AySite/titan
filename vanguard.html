The Vanguard Project is Titan Global's premier foresight platform and startup incubator. We identify, fund, and scale the disruptive technologies that will define the next century of infrastructure.

Two Missions.
One Ecosystem.

To maintain our position as a global leader, we cannot merely adapt to the future; we must invent it. The Vanguard Project operates dual mandates to achieve this:

01

The Foresight Platform

Our internal think-tank dedicated to analyzing macro-trends 20 years into the future. We study demographic shifts, climate volatility, and material scarcity to inform Titan Global's long-term corporate strategy.

02

The Startup Incubator

An intensive accelerator for Prop-Tech and Con-Tech startups. We provide early-stage founders with capital, mentorship, and crucially, access to our global construction sites to test their solutions in the real world.

The Incubator Timeline

How we scale startups from seed to site.

We host biannual global pitch competitions in Paris, New York, and Singapore. Selected startups receive an initial equity-free grant of $100k, alongside dedicated desk space in our regional Innovation Hubs.
This is our differentiator. Startups are paired with a Titan Project Manager and given access to a live construction site or operational concession asset to safely deploy, test, and refine their prototype under real-world conditions.
Following successful pilot validation, the Vanguard Fund steps in as a lead or strategic investor for Seed to Series B rounds, injecting capital between $1M and $15M to accelerate hardware production or software scaling.
The ultimate goal. We onboard the startup's mature technology directly into Titan Global's supply chain as an official vendor, deploying their solution across our multi-billion dollar project portfolio worldwide.
