Our Philanthropic Arm

Titan
Foundation

For over 20 years, we have supported projects of general interest in the territories where our Group operates, focusing on civic engagement and environmental protection.

Social
Solidarity

The Foundation supports initiatives that build social ties and promote civic engagement. We help associations work toward professional integration for people who are far from the labor market, with a focus on construction and logistics trades.

"Our goal is to give every individual the tools to build their own future within the community."

Environmental
Stewardship

Protecting biodiversity and preserving natural resources are central to our philanthropic missions. We fund projects that raise environmental awareness among youth and support local ecological restoration programs.

  • / Urban Forestation
  • / Marine Ecosystem Research
  • / Waste Management Education

Skills
Sponsorship

The Foundation is unique because it relies on the voluntary commitment of Titan Global employees. Our staff provide technical expertise—architecture, engineering, and project management—to the non-profits we support.

5,000+ Employee Volunteers Annually

Requesting Support

The Titan Foundation issues an annual call for projects. We select initiatives based on their long-term social impact, technical feasibility, and alignment with our core values of integrity and solidarity.
