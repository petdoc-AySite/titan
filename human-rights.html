Our Missions & Actions / Human Rights & Ethics

Integrity in Every Action.

Our commitment to human rights and corporate ethics is non-negotiable. As a global leader, we define our success not just by the structures we build, but by the integrity of the path we take to reach them.

Global Governance Framework

At Titan Global, ethics is a collective responsibility. Our governance framework ensures compliance with international standards and fosters a culture of transparency across all subsidiaries.

Titan Global is a signatory of the UN Global Compact. We commit to:

  • Eradicating forced and child labor within our operations and supply chains.
  • Ensuring a safe and healthy working environment for all project staff.
  • Guaranteeing freedom of association and the right to collective bargaining.
  • Eliminating discrimination in respect of employment and occupation.
"We conduct rigorous human rights impact assessments in high-risk zones before project commencement."

Our Code of Ethics applies to every employee, officer, and partner. It establishes zero tolerance for:

Anti-Corruption

Strict prohibition of bribery, facilitation payments, and inappropriate gifts.

Conflicts of Interest

Clear protocols for declaring and mitigating personal or financial conflicts.

Our Vigilance Plan identifies risks and prevents serious violations of human rights and environmental health across our supply chain.

Risk Mapping: Identification of risk areas by business line and geographic location.
Third-Party Evaluation: Systematic audits of our subcontractors and suppliers.
Reporting: Annual monitoring of our risk mitigation measures.

We provide a secure, multilingual platform for employees and stakeholders to report concerns regarding ethics, health, or environmental violations.

Internal Integrity Line

1-800-TITAN-SAFE

Available 24/7. Anonymous reporting available. Zero tolerance for retaliation against whistleblowers.
