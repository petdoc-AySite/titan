Our Geographic Operations

Our decentralized management model empowers regional directors to make decisions closest to the field, ensuring every project respects local environmental and social ecosystems.

Europe

Our Historical Anchor

Generating 44% of total group revenue, Europe remains the testing ground for our most advanced environmental innovations. Our robust concession portfolio here includes over 4,500 km of managed motorways and key airport hubs.

  • HQ in Paris, France
  • Major operations in the UK, Germany, and Spain
  • Pioneering Hydrogen mobility networks

The Americas

Rapid Expansion

A high-growth market driven by industrial reshoring and state-led infrastructure bills. Recent strategic developments include establishing comprehensive logistics and administrative hubs in the Moreno Valley corridor and the Altamont Pass to support heavy civil engineering projects and high-tech manufacturing facilities.

  • Energy grid modernization in North America
  • Inland Empire & California Valley Logistics hubs
  • Transport infrastructure in Latin America

Africa & Middle East

Complex Engineering

Operating in complex environments, we deliver critical infrastructure with a strong focus on water management, transport corridors, and local skills transfer, ensuring our presence leaves a lasting socio-economic legacy.

  • Large-scale water desalination plants
  • Urban rail networks in emerging megacities
  • 95% local hiring initiative

Asia-Pacific

Sustainable Megacities

Focused on providing mobility and energy solutions for rapidly expanding urban populations. Our expertise in deep foundation engineering and smart city grids positions us perfectly for the region's sustainable development goals.

  • Offshore wind energy connections
  • Deep-tunnel public transit systems
  • Smart mobility technology integration
Our Socio-Economic Footprint

A Multi-Local Enterprise

We do not just build infrastructure; we build local capacities. Because construction and energy solutions are inherently tied to their physical location, Titan Global operates as a network of autonomous, highly agile business units.

1

Hiring Locally

Across our international operations, over 90% of our workforce and management teams are hired directly from the host country.

2

Supply Chain Integration

We prioritize local suppliers and SMEs, injecting billions of euros directly into regional economies and reducing the carbon footprint of transport.
