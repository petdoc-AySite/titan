Personal Data

Commitment to Privacy & Data Protection

At Titan Global Construction, the protection of your personal data is a priority. We are committed to processing your information with the highest standards of security and transparency.

1. Scope of Data Collection

When you interact with our platform, we may collect information that identifies you directly or indirectly. This collection occurs through:

  • Contact Forms: Name, professional email, company, and phone number.
  • Recruitment: CVs, cover letters, and professional history submitted via the "Join Us" portal.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and navigation behavior via cookies.

2. Purpose of Processing

Titan Global uses your data strictly for the following purposes:

Service Excellence

Responding to inquiries, managing project bids, and providing technical documentation.

Talent Acquisition

Evaluating applications for our global construction and engineering projects.

3. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal or reporting requirements:

  • Client & Business Data: 5 years after the end of the contractual relationship.
  • Recruitment Data: 2 years after the last contact with the candidate.
  • Technical Logs: 12 months from the date of capture.

4. Your Rights

In accordance with international data protection regulations (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the following rights regarding your information:

Right to Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your data under specific conditions.

5. Security Measures

Titan Global implements rigorous technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. This includes encrypted communications (SSL), restricted access protocols, and regular security audits of our industrial infrastructure.

Contact the Data Protection Officer

For any requests regarding your personal data or to exercise your rights, please contact our global privacy team:

dpo@titanglobalconstructions.com
