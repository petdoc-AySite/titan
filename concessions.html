A Long-Term Partner for Public Mobility

Titan Concessions acts as a long-term partner to public authorities. We manage transport infrastructure under public-private partnerships and concession contracts, assuming the financial risks of building and operating these vital networks over decades.

Our focus is on modernizing infrastructure to address the dual challenges of massive urban growth and the urgent need for ecological transition. From smart tolling systems to decarbonized airports, we are redefining what infrastructure can achieve.

Our Expertise

Three Pillars of Global Connectivity

Highways & Motorways

As the world's leading private motorway operator, we manage extensive networks that are crucial for regional economic vitality. Our current focus is heavily invested in transforming traditional roadways into low-carbon mobility corridors.

  • ✓ Deployment of free-flow toll systems to reduce congestion and emissions.
  • ✓ Installation of ultra-fast electric vehicle (EV) charging stations every 50km.
  • ✓ Preservation of biodiversity via specialized wildlife crossings and eco-bridges.

Titan Airports

Operating an international network spanning the Americas, Europe, and Asia. We aim to offer a seamless passenger experience while committing to achieve net-zero emissions across our entire network by 2050.

  • ✓ Integration of biometric technology for secure and fluid passenger processing.
  • ✓ Massive investments in solar farms to power terminal operations.
  • ✓ Preparation of infrastructure for Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) and hydrogen aircraft.

Railways & Transit

Providing high-capacity, low-carbon mobility alternatives. We operate high-speed rail lines and urban transit systems that drastically cut travel times while minimizing the environmental footprint of mass transport.

  • ✓ Operation and maintenance of over 500 km of high-speed rail networks.
  • ✓ Development of multimodal hubs connecting rail, road, and active transport.
  • ✓ Predictive maintenance driven by AI to ensure 99.9% network availability.
