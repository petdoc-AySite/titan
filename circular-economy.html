ESG Pillar 02

Circular
Economy.

Moving from a "Take-Make-Waste" model to a circular ecosystem. At Titan Global, we view every demolition as a harvest and every site as a resource loop.

Redefining Material Lifecycle

Our circular economy strategy is built on three distinct actions: **Eliminate** waste at the design phase, **Circulate** materials at their highest value, and **Regenerate** the natural systems we operate within.

95% Recovery Rate

We aim to recover and repurpose 95% of inert waste from our demolition and construction activities by 2030.

Recycled Aggregates

Integrating over 2 million tonnes of recycled aggregate into our new road and infrastructure projects annually.

Design Strategy

Design for Deconstruction

We are moving away from permanent chemical bonding toward modular, mechanical connections. This allows future generations to "unzip" our buildings and repurpose structural elements without destruction.

On-Site Logistics

Smart Waste Hubs

Every Titan Global worksite features a digital Waste Hub. Using AI-vision sensors, we sort wood, metal, and concrete at the source, ensuring high-purity streams for immediate recycling.

Resource Loop

The "Urban Mine"

Demolished concrete is crushed and treated on-site using our mobile recycling units. This "Urban Mining" approach eliminates the need for 70% of heavy truck logistics involved in material transport.

Closed-Loop Water Systems

Construction is water-intensive. To combat scarcity, Titan Global has implemented closed-loop filtration systems at all industrial sites. We treat and re-use "grey water" for dust suppression and concrete mixing, reducing our reliance on municipal freshwater by up to 60%.
