ESG Pillar 01

Acting for
the Climate.

Climate change is the defining challenge of our industry. Titan Global is committed to a science-based trajectory to reach Net Zero across our entire value chain by 2050.

2030

Interim
Goal

2050

Net Zero
Target

Decarbonizing
Operations

We are aggressively transitioning our heavy machinery and logistics fleet to electric and hydrogen power. By 2028, 60% of our small-to-medium site vehicles will be zero-emission.

  • Fleet Electrification
  • On-site Solar Arrays
  • LED Retrofitting

Low-Carbon
Materials

Construction materials account for a significant portion of Scope 3 emissions. We are partnering with innovators to deploy ultra-low carbon concrete and recycled steel across all "Class A" industrial projects.

  • Recycled Aggregate
  • Bio-based Insulation
  • Carbon-Injected Concrete

Concession
Efficiency

For the assets we manage—including airports and motorways—we implement energy-saving AI that optimizes lighting, HVAC, and traffic flow to reduce operational footprints by up to 30%.

  • Smart Grid Integration
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • HVAC Heat Recovery
